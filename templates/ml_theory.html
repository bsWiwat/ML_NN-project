Understanding the Heart Disease Dataset and Model Development Process

Dataset Overview

Heart Disease UCI Dataset

Data from : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/johnsmith88/heart-disease-dataset

The Heart Disease UCI dataset is a comprehensive collection of medical data used to predict the presence of heart disease in patients. It combines data from several institutions and includes various clinical and diagnostic measurements.

Dataset Characteristics

  • 1025 patient records
  • 14 clinical attributes
  • Binary classification target
  • Mixed numerical and categorical features

Key Features

  • Age and Gender
  • Chest Pain Type (4 categories)
  • Resting Blood Pressure
  • Serum Cholesterol Levels
  • Resting ECG Results
  • Maximum Heart Rate
